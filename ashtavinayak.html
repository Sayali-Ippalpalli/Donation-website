Ashtavinayak Trust is a non-Government charitable organization at Pune, (Maharashtra state / India) and provides home for orphans

Rooms

The trusts rooms are spacious and well ventilated. Each room accommodates 2 to 4 students depending on the size of the room. Each student is provided with a bed, a mattress, a table, a chair, a cupboard or shelf, light, fan, etc.

Mess

Trust has a spacious dining hall and kitchen. Nutritious and wholesome meals are provided to students in the morning and evening every day. Students themselves are expected to wash their plates after meals and take care of cleanliness in the dining hall.

Details

Trust has 256 beneficiaries orphan students.
Trust is been working since 1990.
Most of students are in age between 15-22
