NIWARA OLD AGE HOME

Here to help the old ones

The companion for old age

NIWARA is self sustained trust which is trusted by the society for its commitments. Niwara as a family, is committed, to support all inmates for basic needs through out their life.

Here's The Deal

"'NIWARA' is a self-supporting trust, that society acknowledges for its dedication to all its residents. Their goals and objectives are to serve the elderly destitute. Their staff and volunteers seek to make their lives as happy and comfortable as possible. The home offers all the services at free of charge for their citizens. They have a modern residential building with 20 rooms, each with eight beds. They celebrate all essential festivals and three times a year all the citizens are presented with new clothes."

Speciality

Fresh Hot Meals, Library, Bhajan Group, Arts and Craft Activities, Visiting Interesting Places, Re-Habitalisation Centre, Physiotherapy, Gymnasium & Community Hally. Students themselves are expected to wash their plates after meals and take care of cleanliness in the dining hall.

Details

NIWARA is a self sustained trust, which is recognised by society for its commitment to all its residents. Our aims and objectives are to serve the elderly destitude. Once admitted in Niwara, all the services are free of charge for our residents . We look after them in every respect throughout their life. Generous people from all walks of life come forward to donate in kind and money, which we gratefully accept.
