Jeevan Ashray Old Age Home

Our Vision

JEEVAN ASHRAY is a "HOME AWAY FROM HOME". It is an institute run by the Mittal Sisters. A quality and respectful living is provided for the retired Senior Citizens by qualified professionals with a dream to create, nurture each others growth in a spirit of transparency, commitment, trust and to promote genuine love and brotherhood.

Our Mission

The entire staff at Jeevan Ashray are committed to the physical, mental, emotional and spiritual well being of senior citizens in order to live a comfortable life despite their age. Above all to provide the care, comfort and security so essential for a life of dignity and self esteem. To inspire every member of this home to attain fullness of life and everlasting joy.

Benefits

It is one of the safest place to live in. To look upon retirement not as an end but as a new beginning. It is a beginning of an active chapter of your life where you can deploy the vast reservoir of knowledge and skills for a better life for yourself and for society, making a positive contribution that will make you feel not only younger but also proud.To create and build up new friendship among the residence whose moral support will strengthen you.To share experiences with other residence.
